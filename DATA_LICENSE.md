## Notes & caveats

- The structured register data is released under **CC0 1.0**, so it may be freely
  used, modified, combined and redistributed — including commercially — with no
  copyright obligation.
- The data contains **personal data** (politicians' names, biographies, votes,
  side-income disclosures). CC0 waives *copyright*, not data-protection law:
  GDPR/DSGVO obligations still apply to how you process and publish it,
  independent of the licence.
- Free-text/HTML fields (e.g. a poll's `field_intro`, a side job's
  `additional_information`) may quote third-party material; use within the
  bounds of applicable law.
- CC0 covers the **data**. The abgeordnetenwatch.de **website, logo and branding**
  are not part of this dedication.
